# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple contributor covenant:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but prioritize user safety and payment correctness.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the skill and Rute Bayar contributor workflows.
- Do not publish secrets, credentials, or private operational details.

Maintainers may remove comments or contributions that are abusive, unsafe, spammy, or unrelated to the project.
